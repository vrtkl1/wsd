Wooden surfboards are pretty rare these days. Bruce has recently started shaping and building custom wooden boards and the first one is almost done. All it needs is a logo and glass and it will be ready to ride.

The First Board

The board is 10 feet long and made out of cedar. It is a classic noseriding longboard shape.It looks like it should be pretty fun to ride once its done. Bruce has a couple pieces of redwood that he built into the tailblock that give a little extra style.
